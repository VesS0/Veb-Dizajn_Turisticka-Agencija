AQUARIUS 3*

Location: Budva

It is located in the center of Budva. The distance from the sea is 100 meters away from the administrative center of the city and 150 meters from the old town 400 meters.   Aquarius Hotel has double, triple rooms, studios for two persons and two apartments for 2 to 4 people. Each room and suite has air conditioning, LCD TV, refrigerator, mini bar, safe, balcony and wi-fi internet.   On the ground floor there is a restaurant that has a tradition of more than two decades. It is built in a Mediterranean style and equipped with antique coastal detail. The restaurant has a floral terrace, where the sounds of Mediterranean music and a large assortment of fish and national specialties and an extensive wine list, guests can experience the magic moments in a pleasant atmosphere.
